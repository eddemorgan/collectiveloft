Collective Loft is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have regarding your data. By using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Section 01

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use the Platform, you provide us with:

  • Name and email address
  • Password (stored encrypted — we do not have access to your plaintext password)
  • Location (country, state, city)
  • Creative profile information: disciplines, skills, bio, headline, Right Now card, portfolio links, social links
  • Collaboration preferences: seeking disciplines and skills
  • Content you upload: portfolio files, studio files, profile images, cover images
  • Messages and communications within Loft Studios
  • Collaboration terms you create or accept
  • Ratings and reviews you submit
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — see Section 4)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Platform, we and our service providers automatically collect:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, referring URLs
  • Device information: device type, operating system, browser version
  • Usage data: features used, actions taken, search queries, collaboration activity
  • Session data: authentication tokens managed by Supabase Auth
  • Analytics data: page views, navigation patterns, feature engagement (via Google Analytics)

1.3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Collective Loft uses the following cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Authentication cookies: set by Supabase Auth to maintain your logged-in session. Required for Platform functionality. Cannot be disabled while using the Platform.
  • Google Analytics cookies: used to understand how users navigate and use the Platform. These cookies do not identify you personally. You may opt out via browser settings or the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on (tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioral targeting technologies beyond those described above.

Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide, operate, and improve the Platform
  • Power the Discipline Matching algorithm
  • Enable collaboration features (Briefs, Terms, Loft Studios)
  • Process subscription payments through Stripe
  • Send transactional emails related to your account and collaborations
  • Display your profile and creative identity to other users
  • Generate and display Community Voice ratings and reviews
  • Analyze Platform usage to improve features and performance
  • Respond to support requests and resolve disputes
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the security and integrity of the Platform

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data to serve third-party advertising.

Section 03

How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

Collective Loft is a professional network. The following profile information is visible to other users by default:

  • Your name, headline, bio, and Right Now card
  • Your disciplines, skills, and collaboration preferences
  • Your location (city, state, country)
  • Your portfolio links and social links
  • Your collaboration history (count and project titles from completed Loft Studios)
  • Your Community Voice rating and reviews

Information shared within a Loft Studio (messages, files, notes, milestones) is visible only to the two parties in that Studio. It is not visible to other users.

3.2 With Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the Platform:

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Supabase Database, auth, storage, realtime All platform data — hosted and processed on Supabase infrastructure
Vercel Platform hosting and deployment Server logs, request data
Stripe Subscription payment processing Name, email, payment method details
Resend Transactional email delivery Name, email, notification content
Google Analytics Platform usage analytics Anonymized usage data, device info, page views

Each of these providers has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data. We encourage you to review their policies.

3.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your information when required by law, subpoena, court order, or government request, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of Collective Loft, our users, or the public.

Section 04

Payment Data

Collective Loft does not store your full payment card information. All payment processing is handled by Stripe. When you subscribe, your payment details are transmitted directly to Stripe and governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy (stripe.com/privacy).

We retain a record of your subscription status, billing history, and the last four digits of your card on file as provided by Stripe for account management purposes.

Section 05

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Active account data: retained for the life of your account
  • Completed Loft Studio records: retained as part of both parties' collaboration history, even after account cancellation, because a collaboration belongs to both participants. When a user departs the Platform, Collective Loft will handle data removal requests on a case-by-case basis, including facilitating the transfer of applicable project files to any remaining active collaborator before removing the departed user's personal data where feasible and required by law
  • Messages and files within Studios: retained as part of the Studio record
  • Billing records: retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • System backups: purged within 90 days on a rolling basis
  • Google Analytics data: retained per Google's standard retention policies (typically 26 months)

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your profile data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal compliance, active disputes, or completed collaboration records tied to another user's history.

Section 06

Your Rights & Choices

Access & Correction

You may access and update most of your profile information directly through your account settings and profile edit mode at any time.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account by contacting hello@collectiveloft.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Note that some data may be retained as described in Section 5.

Google Analytics Opt-Out

You may opt out of Google Analytics data collection by installing the opt-out browser add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout, or by adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

Marketing Email Opt-Out

You may opt out of non-essential marketing communications at any time through your account settings or the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.

6.5 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact hello@collectiveloft.com.

6.6 European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or applicable local law, including rights of access, rectification, erasure, portability, and objection. Our legal basis for processing your data is primarily contract performance (to provide the Platform you subscribed to) and legitimate interests (to operate and improve the Platform). To exercise your rights, contact hello@collectiveloft.com.

Section 07

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted password storage (managed by Supabase Auth)
  • Row Level Security (RLS) enforced at the database level
  • Access controls limiting which staff can access user data

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or maintain personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a user under 18, we will delete that information and terminate the account immediately.

If you believe a user under 18 has created an account, please contact us at hello@collectiveloft.com.

Section 09

Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites, portfolio platforms, social media profiles, and external content. This Privacy Policy applies only to the Collective Loft Platform. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing personal information.
